Our Story

Building the future of cabinet manufacturing in the Charlotte metro area

Launching October 2025

After years of experience in precision manufacturing and a deep understanding of contractor needs in the Charlotte area, we're launching Peppermodern to address a critical gap in the market: fast, reliable, professional cabinet part production.

Founded in 2014 with a vision of bringing modern CNC technology to traditional cabinet making, we recognized that contractors needed a partner who could deliver precision-cut components without the typical weeks-long wait times.

Our October 2025 launch represents the culmination of careful planning, equipment selection, and process development designed around one simple promise: from consultation to finished parts in 7 days.
