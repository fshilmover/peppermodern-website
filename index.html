CNC Milling Services

Sheet Processing

$75 per sheet

We will nest and cut panels from Optimizer files from Mozaik CNC software. We do not offer the ability for customers to provide g-code nor work with other CAD/CAM software other than Aspire using our drafting services.

  • 3-Axis CNC precision cutting
  • Professional labeling
  • Waste disposal included
  • Mozaik CNC software compatibility
  • 7-day turnaround from consultation

Edge Banding

$1 per linear foot

We will edge band your parts using edgebanding that you provide up to 1mm in thickness. Upcharge for 2mm is $0.05 per linear foot and 3mm is $0.10 per linear foot.

  • Pre-milling preparation
  • End trimming
  • Edge milling: flat, chamfer, 2mm roundover
  • Edge scraping: 1mm roundover
  • Corner rounding and flat scraping
  • Professional nesting optimization

Drafting Services

$150 per hour

We will custom design your project using our library of modern products and construction methods in Mozaik CNC or create custom parts in Vectric V-Carve or Aspire.

  • Room and product layout design
  • Mozaik CNC integration
  • Vectric V-Carve/Aspire custom parts
  • Modern construction methods
  • Professional project planning

Panel Layup

$100 per sheet

Layup of customer supplied rolled veneer on substrate panels. Price is per sheet per side. Adhesive included.

  • Vacuum press application
  • Professional adhesive application
  • Customer-supplied veneer
  • Quality finishing
